# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Homeowners, shop owners, school administrators, and office managers across Botswana who need reliable, hands-on technology support. They find TechLab Botswana through word of mouth, WhatsApp, or local search. They want someone local who can fix laptops, install CCTV, set up POS systems, and build websites without corporate jargon or long wait times.

## Product Purpose

TechLab Botswana's website is a scroll-based brand surface that communicates trust, craft, and nationwide reach from a Mahalapye base. Success means a visitor understands what TechLab does, feels confident contacting them, and can request a quote or WhatsApp message within one scroll session.

## Brand Personality

Practical, local, trustworthy, hands-on. TechLab speaks like a skilled technician who explains things clearly — not a SaaS startup, not a luxury agency. The tone is **direct** (what we do, where we are, how to reach us), **grounded** (real workshop, real installs, real support), and **warm** (community-rooted, patient with non-technical customers).

Three-word personality: **practical, local, trustworthy**.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI SaaS landing pages: purple gradients, glassmorphism, neon glows, Inter font, identical icon-card grids
- Editorial magazine aesthetics: italic display serifs, broadsheet grids, Fraunces/Playfair typography
- Numbered section scaffolding (01 / 02 / 03) as default grammar
- Stock-photo filler without narrative connection to Botswana IT work
- Hedging copy: "we might help", "consider reaching out"

## Design Principles

1. **Show the work.** Documentary-style imagery of repairs, installs, and setups — not abstract tech illustrations alone.
2. **Scroll as narrative.** Each section reveals one idea; motion supports reading, not decoration.
3. **Local first.** Mahalapye base, nationwide reach — geography and community are part of the story.
4. **Accessible craft.** High contrast, readable type, reduced-motion respect — professional without being flashy.
5. **One clear action.** WhatsApp and quote form are always within reach.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA contrast on all text
- Keyboard-navigable nav and form
- `prefers-reduced-motion` disables scroll parallax and heavy animation
- Descriptive alt text on all images
- Semantic HTML with ARIA only where needed
